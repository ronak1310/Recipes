Veg Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter for toasting
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Mayonnaise or ketchup

Instructions:

  1. Mix mashed potatoes, vegetables, cornflour, spices, and salt.
  2. Shape into patties and shallow-fry until golden.
  3. Toast the burger buns with butter.
  4. Spread mayonnaise or ketchup on the buns.
  5. Place lettuce, tomato, patty, and cheese between the buns.
  6. Serve hot with fries or a drink.
