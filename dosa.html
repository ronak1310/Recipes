Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For Dosa Batter:
  • 2 cups rice
  • ½ cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • ¼ cup poha (flattened rice)
  • ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • For Cooking Dosa:
  • Oil or ghee (for greasing the pan)
  • Optional Fillings:
  • Masala Potato Filling (for Masala Dosa)
  • Chutney & Sambar for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Batter:
  2. Rinse rice, urad dal, poha, and fenugreek seeds. Soak them in water for 6 hours.
  3. Grind into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  4. Transfer to a bowl, cover, and let it ferment overnight (8-12 hours).
  5. Add salt and mix well before making dosas.
  6. Make the Dosa:
  7. Heat a non-stick or cast-iron pan. Lightly grease with oil.
  8. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it in a circular motion.
  9. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  10. If using a filling, add it to the center before folding.
  11. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
