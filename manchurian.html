Veg Manchurian Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For Manchurian Balls:
  • 1 cup cabbage (finely chopped)
  • ½ cup carrot (grated)
  • ½ cup capsicum (finely chopped)
  • ¼ cup spring onions (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp black pepper powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Oil (for frying)
  • For Manchurian Sauce:
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger (minced)
  • ½ cup onion (finely chopped)
  • ½ cup capsicum (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • ½ tsp black pepper powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cornflour mixed with ¼ cup water (for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. Make the Manchurian Balls:
  2. In a bowl, mix cabbage, carrot, capsicum, spring onions, cornflour, all-purpose flour, soy sauce, black pepper, and salt.
  3. Shape into small balls.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry the balls until golden brown. Drain and set aside.
  5. Prepare the Sauce:
  6. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  7. Add chopped onions and capsicum, stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  8. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, chili sauce, vinegar, black pepper, and salt. Mix well.
  9. Pour in cornflour slurry and stir until the sauce thickens.
  10. Combine & Serve:
  11. Add the fried Manchurian balls to the sauce and mix well.
  12. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot with fried rice or noodles.
