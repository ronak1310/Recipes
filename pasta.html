Veg Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic and onions, sauté until golden.
  3. Add vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree, chili flakes, oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add cooked pasta and mix well.
  7. Top with grated cheese and serve hot.
