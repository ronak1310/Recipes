Spinach & Walnut Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta of choice
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • ½ cup walnuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, blend spinach, walnuts, garlic, and olive oil until smooth.
  3. Add Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Blend again.
  4. Mix the pesto sauce with the cooked pasta.
  5. Serve warm with extra Parmesan on top.
