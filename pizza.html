Veg Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza base
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • ½ cup onions (sliced)
  • ½ cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • ¼ cup olives (sliced)
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F).
  2. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the base.
  3. Add grated mozzarella cheese.
  4. Top with bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and olives.
  5. Sprinkle oregano, chili flakes, and a little salt.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the cheese melts and the crust is golden.
  7. Slice and serve hot.
